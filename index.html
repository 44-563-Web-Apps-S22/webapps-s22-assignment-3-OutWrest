Directions to Senor Burrito from Kansas City Internation Airport

  1. Take the I-29 North past St. Joseph.
  2. Take exit 56A toward US-71.
  3. Turn left onto Main Street in Maryville.
  4. Senor Burrito will be on right across from US Bank.
